Autonomous Lead Qualification

AI system that handles the full SDR function across email and LinkedIn: classifies replies, qualifies leads through multi-turn conversation, responds with product knowledge, and follows up autonomously.

The Situation

The demand generation engine runs campaigns across email and LinkedIn. Every reply needs classification (interested? bad fit?), qualification (team size? CRM?), a contextual response, CRM updates, and follow-up scheduling. Doing this manually at scale is the bottleneck. Hiring SDRs is expensive and slow. The question: can AI handle the entire function, not just draft replies, but qualify, route, and follow up autonomously?

The System

An AI pipeline that processes every incoming reply end-to-end:

  1. Classify
    Every incoming reply gets instant intent classification. Interested leads move forward. Declines, bad fits, and automated responses get filtered and logged without wasting anyone's time.
  2. Detect escalation triggers
    Checks if the lead wants a live demo or provided a referral contact. Only these get escalated to a human via Slack with full context. Everything else stays in the AI pipeline.
  3. Draft contextual response
    Two sub-agents work together: one queries the product knowledge base for accurate pricing, features, and integration details. The other drafts a response grounded in the full conversation history.
  4. Qualify through conversation
    Extracts qualification signals from natural conversation across multiple turns. Qualified leads get a demo booking link. If the system can't determine fit after multiple exchanges, it escalates to a human instead of guessing.
  5. Follow up autonomously
    Replies to the original thread, logs everything, and schedules follow-ups on an escalating cadence. Checks CRM status before every action — never emails someone who's already converted or been closed.
